Variable: ClassInfo
This variable just to inform the researchers that we represent Legitimate and Spam classes in Folds and FullSMStext variables by 1 and 2, respectively.


Variable: Folds
The 10 used folds in the paper. The first column in each row represents the 9 training folds, while the second column for the test fold.


Variable: FullSMStext
The final English SMS collection from which we created the folds.
